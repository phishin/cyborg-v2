FAQs

What types of logos can I create with Cyborg?

Cyborg specializes in industry-specific logos tailored for businesses in sectors like construction. You can choose from various design styles, from minimalist to intricate, to best represent your brand.

How long does it take to get my logo?

After you select and purchase your logo, you can expect to receive the finalized files ready for download within 24 hours.

What if I'm not satisfied with the initial logo options?

If the initial options don’t meet your needs, you can provide feedback and refine the choices through our feedback loop, ensuring the final design aligns perfectly with your vision.

Can I modify a logo after I have selected it?

Yes, minor modifications are allowed before finalizing your purchase to ensure the logo matches your business requirements exactly.

What file formats will I receive my logo in?

Your logo will be delivered in multiple formats to ensure versatility across different uses, including vector EPS, PDF, SVG for scalability, and raster PNGs in various colors and variations, appropriate to your purchased package.

Do I have full rights to the logo once purchased?

Upon purchase, you can use the logo for any business need. However, due to the semi-unique nature of our template-based design process, other businesses may have similar designs.

Can I copyright the logo?

While you are free to use your purchased logo in any way that benefits your business, it's important to note that copyrighting a logo derived from a semi-custom template may not be necessary or advantageous for many businesses. In fact, the majority of logos, especially in service industries, are not copyrighted because the direct commercial benefits, such as resale of the logo itself, are minimal. Instead, the value of a logo lies in its ability to uniquely identify and enhance your business's brand in the marketplace. If you have specific legal concerns or if your business model includes broader commercial uses of the logo, consulting with a legal professional can provide guidance tailored to your situation.

Can these files be used for large format printing?

Yes, the vector EPS format included with your logo is ideal for large format printing, as it can be resized without any loss of quality.

Can I have a custom logo made?

Yes, if our pre-designed options do not fully meet your needs, you can request a completely custom logo. Please fill out the form on our website to get a tailored quote.
